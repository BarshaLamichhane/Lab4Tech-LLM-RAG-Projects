We are currently looking for an AI Engineer responsible for designing, developing, and deploying intelligent AI agents and multi-agent systems. The role focuses on building autonomous and specialized agents that leverage large language models (LLMs), retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and orchestration techniques to automate workflows and deliver business-driven solutions.



What are the requirements?

Strong proficiency in Python, including asynchronous programming patterns for building scalable and efficient AI workflows
Proven experience designing and deploying LLM-based agents in production environments, including reasoning, tool use, and workflow orchestration
Hands-on experience with agent frameworks such as LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI or similar
Solid understanding of RAG architectures, including embeddings, vector databases, chunking, and retrieval strategies
Experience with managed AI platforms (AWS Bedrock, Microsoft Foundry) and integration with APIs (REST / GraphQL)
Familiarity with containerized environments (Docker/Kubernetes), Infrastructure as Code, and observability practices (logging, tracing, monitoring)


What will your responsibilities be? 

Design, develop, and deploy autonomous AI agents leveraging large language models (LLMs), including reasoning workflows, planning logic, prompt engineering strategies, and memory architectures (short‑term and long‑term)
Build and maintain retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines, connecting agents to internal knowledge bases, document repositories, APIs, and enterprise data sources
Design and orchestrate multi-agent systems, implementing coordination patterns (sequential, parallel, hierarchical) and optimizing workflows for performance, cost, and reliability
Integrate AI agents into enterprise platforms and managed services (e.g. AWS Bedrock, Microsoft Foundry), designing scalable and hybrid architectures
Ensure AI safety, governance, and compliance, including guardrails, monitoring, observability pipelines, and alignment with GDPR and data protection requirements
Collaborate with business and research teams to identify use cases, translate requirements into technical solutions, and deliver production-ready AI applications
