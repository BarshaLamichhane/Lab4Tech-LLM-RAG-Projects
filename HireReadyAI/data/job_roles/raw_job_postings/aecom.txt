Role Description 
At AECOM, we're building a new kind of AI Engineering team — one that pairs the speed and ownership of a startup with the scale and real-world reach of one of the world's leading infrastructure firms. Based in Zug, we turn everyday operational and business challenges into intelligent, production-grade systems: the workflows, internal tools, automations, and applications that connect across the platforms our global organisation runs on — shipped fast and built to last.
You'll work shoulder to shoulder with business and engineering teams — scoping a real problem, prototyping a solution in days rather than months, and deploying it where it makes a measurable difference. It's a deeply hands-on role, grounded in frontier models, modern orchestration frameworks, and serious software engineering craft.
You'll design and ship:
Enterprise AI agents and internal copilots 
Workflow automations and orchestration systems 
AI-powered knowledge and retrieval systems 
AI-powered workflows integrated across enterprise platforms and internal systems
