About the job
This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for a Machine Learning Engineer in Luxembourg.

This role offers an opportunity to work at the forefront of applied artificial intelligence, designing and deploying scalable machine learning systems in a fully remote, global environment. You will contribute to the development of high-performance AI/ML pipelines and services that power production-grade applications across diverse domains. The position involves working with cloud-native architectures and modern AI frameworks, including large language models and deep learning systems. You will be part of an international engineering team that values autonomy, innovation, and ownership of end-to-end solutions. The environment encourages experimentation, continuous learning, and hands-on problem solving on mission-critical systems. This is an ideal role for engineers passionate about building robust, scalable, and intelligent systems that operate at production scale.

Accountabilities

Design, develop, and maintain scalable AI/ML services and pipelines in cloud-based environments.
Build and optimize machine learning workflows, including data preparation, curation, annotation, training, validation, and deployment.
Contribute to research and development initiatives involving emerging AI frameworks, models, and technologies.
Deploy and support production-grade machine learning systems with a strong focus on reliability, scalability, and performance.
Troubleshoot and resolve issues in mission-critical production environments following an end-to-end ownership mindset (“you build it, you run it”).
Develop vision-based AI solutions, including object detection, image segmentation, labeling, and classification tasks.
Collaborate with cross-functional and distributed teams to deliver high-quality, production-ready AI solutions.
Continuously improve system architecture, model performance, and operational efficiency through experimentation and optimization.

Requirements

3+ years of hands-on experience in Python development.
4+ years of experience in machine learning, deep learning, or large language model-based systems.
Strong experience working with cloud platforms (preferably AWS) for building and deploying AI/ML pipelines.
Experience designing scalable workflows for data ingestion, processing, labeling, and secure storage for model training.
Proven experience in training, fine-tuning, testing, and deploying machine learning models in production environments.
Solid understanding of computer vision techniques such as object detection, segmentation, and image classification.
Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to design scalable, fault-tolerant, and efficient systems.
Experience working independently and collaboratively in distributed, remote-first teams.
Excellent communication skills for effective collaboration across global engineering teams.
Familiarity with tools such as AWS SageMaker, AWS Bedrock, or NVIDIA AI platforms is a plus.
Exposure to media, audio, video, or image processing workflows is considered an advantage.
